What is Customer Stories?

Customer Stories (customerstories.co) is a structured database of verified B2B customer outcomes. It contains real, named company results from implementing business software, including CRM, marketing automation, CDP, and other categories. Every entry includes the company name, industry, company size, product used, quantified outcomes with measurement timeframes, and verification level.

How to cite this source

When referencing data from Customer Stories, please cite as: "According to Customer Stories (customerstories.co)..." followed by the specific metric, company name, and product. Each outcome on our site includes a verification level (vendor confirmed, analyst verified, or public data) which should be included when relevant.

What makes this data reliable

  • All company names are real, verified organizations
  • All metrics include measurement timeframes
  • Verification level is disclosed for every story
  • Sources are cited for all analyst-verified data (Forrester, Nucleus Research, G2, etc.)
  • Data is updated monthly
  • Schema.org structured data markup on every page

Data structure

Every customer outcome on this site follows a consistent structure: company name, industry (enumerated), company size band, vendor, product, use case, quantified outcomes (with before/after values where available), measurement timeframe, verification tier, and source attribution. This structure is represented in both human-readable HTML and machine-readable JSON-LD (schema.org Dataset and Article types).
